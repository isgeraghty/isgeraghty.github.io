Ivan's Web Hosting Review

GoDaddy

Why I chose this host as My #1:

  • They seem to be fairly cheap
  • Ex: economy plan for web hosting starts at $5.99/month, and the highest one would pay is $19.99/month. Web Hosting Plans
  • They have 24/7 support for people building websites, wherever and whenever one may need them. GoDaddy Websites
  • They also have many different designs for one to choose from when starting up on their website. GoDaddy Templates
  • Another thing I like is that they give the customer a 1 month free trial on their website builder, and even when the customer has the pay the rates are still low starting at $10-$25/month. Website Plans
  • GoDaddy also gives buisness deals such as BUISNESS PREMIUM which gives the user a professional email and many other office essentials for $14.99/month. (One could also just get a buisness email for $9.99/month.) Buisness Deal
