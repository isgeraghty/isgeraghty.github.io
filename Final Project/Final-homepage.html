The Amazingly Dangerous world of Goalkeeping

Goalkeeping is a position in soccer that is very fun but also can be very challenging. There are a lot of fun parts to it but there is also a lot of work to get to the fun parts of it. If you like getting dirty, jumping around, and maybe getting kicked with a ball or by a cleat into your body, then Goalkeeping is for you!
